5 Bedroom Mistakes That
Drain Your Energy While You Sleep

{{ contact.FIRSTNAME | default: "Hi" }}, your bedroom should be a sanctuary — but most people unknowingly turn it into an energy drain.

The 5 Energy Killers

1

Mirror facing the bed

Mirrors bounce energy around the room all night. Your subconscious registers movement, disrupting deep sleep. Fix: Cover it at night or reposition so it doesn't reflect the bed.
2

Bed under a window

No solid wall behind your head = no support. In Feng Shui, the headboard position represents stability in life. Fix: Place the bed against a solid wall with a sturdy headboard.
3

Electronics everywhere

TVs, phones, and laptops carry "active" Fire energy — the opposite of what you need for rest. Fix: Create a charging station outside the bedroom, or at minimum 6 feet from your pillow.
4

Clutter under the bed

Stagnant Qi collects under stored items and rises into your body while you sleep. Old letters, shoes, workout gear — all carry unresolved energy. Fix: Clear it completely. Let air circulate.
5

Door directly aligned with bed

Known as the "coffin position" — Qi rushes straight at you. This creates subconscious anxiety. Fix: Angle the bed or place a piece of furniture between the door line and the bed.

💡 BONUS TIP

The ideal bedroom colors for {{ contact.ELEMENT | default: "your element" }}? Soft, muted earth tones. Avoid bright red (too much Fire) and stark white (too much Metal) — both disrupt sleep.
