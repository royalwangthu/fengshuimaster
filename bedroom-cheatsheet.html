The Commanding Position

Your bed placement determines whether your nervous system enters deep restorative sleep or stays in shallow vigilance mode. The commanding position activates your parasympathetic nervous system — the body's "rest and repair" mode.

  • Bed diagonal from door, not in direct line
  • Headboard against solid wall
  • Can see doorway from pillow
  • No mirror reflecting the bed
  • Space clear under the bed
  • No sharp corners pointing at bed
Quick Test
Lie in bed tonight. Can you see the door without turning your head more than 45°? Is your back against a solid wall? If both answers are yes, you're in the commanding position. If not, this is your #1 change.
Can't Move the Bed?
Place a small mirror on the opposite wall, angled to reflect the doorway. Your peripheral vision will register it, and your nervous system will accept the compromise.

Five Element Color Guide for Bedrooms

🌿
Wood
Sage, moss, forest green. For those feeling stuck or stagnant. Detoxifies emotional energy. Avoid if overstimulated.
🔥
Fire
Dusty rose, peach, blush. For emotional numbness. One candle = medicine, ten = fever. Use sparingly.
🏔️
Earth ★ Best
Beige, clay, sand, terracotta. The #1 bedroom element. Grounds without heaviness. Calms without numbing.
⚔️
Metal
White, silver, soft gold. For mental clutter and racing thoughts. Creates space between thoughts. The element of release.
💧
Water
Charcoal, midnight blue. For spiritual dryness. Powerful but use with care — too much creates groundlessness.

Quick Remedies by Position

Wu Gui — The Haunting

Brass bell or singing bowl near entrance. Ring once before sleep. Metal exhausts fire energy.

Liu Sha — The Unraveling

Rose quartz or amethyst on nightstand. Add paired objects (two candles) for relationship healing.

Jue Ming — The Emptying

Brass Wu Lou (gourd) beside bed + earth-tone bedding. Most urgent — consider changing rooms.

Huo Hai — Small Storms

Small potted plant with rounded leaves (jade plant, peace lily). Composting irritation into growth.

Tonight's Checklist

  • Move bed to commanding position (diagonal from door, back to wall)
  • Remove or cover any mirror facing the bed
  • Clear everything from under the bed
  • Remove electronics or cover them with cloth
  • Add one earth-element object (ceramic, stone, crystal)
  • Close the bedroom door while sleeping
  • Set intention: "This room supports my healing"
